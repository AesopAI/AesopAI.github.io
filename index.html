AesopAgent: Agent-driven Evolutionary System on Story-to-Video Production

Abstract

The Agent and AIGC (Artificial Intelligence Generated Content) technology have recently made significant progress. We introduce AesopAgent, an Agent-driven Evolutionary System on Story-to-Video Production. AesopAgent transforms user story proposals into scripts, images, and audio, which are then harmoniously integrated into a video with special effects. The process orchestration technique is employed to optimize the system workflow for multimodal generation. This ensures that the output video is plentiful and coherent. Our system can autonomously convert user story proposals into narrative videos using a well-orchestrated process. AesopAgent is a practical application of agent technology for multimodal content generation, including a Horizontal Layer and a Utility Layer.

In the Horizontal Layer, we propose two types of Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG) techniques (i.e., Knowledge-RAG and Experience-RAG) to optimize the system workflow. The Knowledge-RAG utilizes expert knowledge to guide generating richly plotted scripts and determining the necessary utilities to be employed. Meanwhile, we obtain efficient workflow, robust system prompts, and illustrative image descriptions through the Experience-RAG, informed by summarizing preliminary system outputs and expert suggestions with LLM (Large Language Model). In the Utility Layer, we assemble multiple utilities based on the fundamental capabilities of each functional unit. We also optimize the usage of these functional units based on feedback information. It ensures that the generated images are stable and rational in terms of composition, character, and style. The generated contents (including scripts, images, and audio) are assembled into expressive and coherent videos. Overall, our system achieves state-of-the-art performance compared with many previous works.

Figure 1: Overview of AesopAgent. This system would convert the user story proposal into a video assembled with images, audio, narration, and special effects. The workflow suggested by AesopAgent, utilizing agent-based approaches and RAG techniques, encompasses script generation, image generation, and video assembly.

Our AesopAgent utilizes agent-based approaches and RAG techniques, coupled with the incorporation of expert insights, to facilitate an iterative evolutionary process that results in an efficient workflow. This workflow enables the automatic creation of high-quality videos from user story proposals. As illustrated in Figure 1, upon receiving a user's ``Dragon Story'' proposal, AesopAgent employs the well-designed workflow by agents implemented with RAGs, including script generation, image generation, and video assembly, ultimately generates a high-quality dragon story video.

Figure 2: Illustration of the AesopAgent framework. The bottom part of the figure shows the workflow from the user story proposal to the video, and the top part shows the main components of our method: the Horizontal Layer and the Utility Layer. The Horizontal Layer is responsible for leveraging agent and RAG techniques, optimizing workflow, generating scripts, and optimizing utilities usage, and the Utility Layer is responsible for providing utilities for image generation and video assembly steps.
